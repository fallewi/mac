Advanced Attribute Filters

Advanced Attribute Filters helps to improve the customer experience on your odoo store.Your customers love to find their desired products and choose products collection to save their time.


List of Features


  • Display Attribute Categories wise filters to group by attribute filter.
  • Show product counts of filtered options.
  • Display applied filtered list on top and remove applied filter selection.
  • Admin can manage all options to display on the product listing (Shop) page.
